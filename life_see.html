血铸长城

Blood-Forged Great Wall

Oil painting & calligraphy, 2025
Oil on canvas, 16 × 20 in
Calligraphy, four-foot format (two sheets)

诗文原作

烽火吞天地，龙蛇撼九陔。
雄关铁骨铸，碧血暮云开。
弹洞残垣在，刀痕砺石皑。
江山凭谁守？兆庶戍轮台。
旌旗卷虺蜮，鼓角荡蒿莱。
华夏存浩气，长风万里来。

Read Translation

Flames devour the earth and sky,
Dragons coil, and ninefold lands sigh.
Iron bones forge the fortress high,
Crimson blood parts the dusk-clouds nigh.

Bullet scars still mar the wall,
Blades have ground the stones in thrall.
Who shall guard the realm today?
Millions stand at watch, arrayed.

Banners roll against the vile,
War drums sweep through weed and isle.
China’s spirit soars untamed—
Ten-thousand miles the long wind flames.
